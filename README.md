# Agentic AI Asset Tracker for Air National Guard

## Overview

This project was developed at the University of Washington Tacoma by
Mahamed Ali and Jason Truong. The goal was to address knowledge
retention and training challenges in Air National Guard (ANG) IT Asset
Management by creating AI-powered guidance tools.

## Problem

ANG personnel face challenges with: - Low information retention - No
centralized knowledge base - Ineffective annual training - Asset
management knowledge gaps

## Solution

We developed and compared three AI-powered prototypes: - ChatGPT Custom
GPT - Google Gemini Gems - Zapier Custom Chatbot

The prototypes provide guidance for: - Asset acquisition - Asset
disposition - Asset transfers - Inventories - IT asset management
procedures - DAFMAN 17-1203 compliance

## Technologies Used

-   ChatGPT Custom GPT
-   Google Gemini Gems
-   Zapier Custom Chatbot
-   AI Prompt Engineering
-   IT Asset Management (ITAM)
-   DAFMAN 17-1203

## Key Contributions

-   Built AI-powered knowledge management prototypes.
-   Designed workflow guidance for IT asset management processes.
-   Evaluated AI platforms based on customization, integration, and
    usability.
-   Created process flowcharts and documentation.
-   Researched Air Force IT Asset Management requirements.

## Results

-   Improved access to asset management guidance.
-   Demonstrated how AI can reduce training gaps.
-   Compared strengths and weaknesses of multiple AI platforms.
